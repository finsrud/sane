# ACTION BY WRITTEN CONSENT OF THE STOCKHOLDERS

[**Note: Certain resolutions may require additional provisions or Board approval. Counsel should review for completeness and any state-specific requirements.**]

## WRITTEN CONSENT TO ACTION WITHOUT MEETING

TO THE STOCKHOLDERS OF [COMPANY NAME], a Delaware corporation (the "Company"):

The undersigned stockholders of the Company (the "Stockholders"), constituting the holders of at least a majority of the outstanding shares of capital stock of the Company entitled to vote thereon, hereby consent to the taking of the following actions without a meeting of the stockholders of the Company in accordance with Section 228 of the General Corporation Law of the State of Delaware (the "DGCL"):

---

## RESOLUTIONS

### FIRST RESOLVED

WHEREAS, the Board of Directors of the Company has determined that it is in the best interests of the Company and its stockholders to amend and restate the Certificate of Incorporation of the Company to authorize the issuance of Series Seed Preferred Stock and to establish the rights, preferences, privileges and restrictions thereof;

NOW THEREFORE, BE IT RESOLVED, that the Restated Certificate of Incorporation of the Company, in substantially the form attached as Exhibit C to the Series Seed Preferred Stock Investment Agreement dated as of [DATE] (the "Investment Agreement"), is hereby approved and adopted, and the officers of the Company are hereby authorized to file the same with the Secretary of State of the State of Delaware.

### SECOND RESOLVED

WHEREAS, the Board of Directors of the Company has determined that it is advisable and in the best interests of the Company and its stockholders to authorize the issuance and sale of shares of Series Seed Preferred Stock of the Company to the purchasers identified in the Investment Agreement, on the terms and conditions set forth therein;

NOW THEREFORE, BE IT RESOLVED, that the issuance and sale of [NUMBER OF SHARES] shares of Series Seed Preferred Stock of the Company at a price of $[PRICE PER SHARE] per share, for a total consideration of $[TOTAL INVESTMENT AMOUNT], to the purchasers named in Schedule 1 to the Investment Agreement, is hereby authorized and approved, and the officers of the Company are hereby authorized to execute and deliver the Investment Agreement and all documents and agreements contemplated thereby and to take all actions necessary to effectuate the transactions contemplated hereby.

### THIRD RESOLVED

WHEREAS, it is necessary and desirable to authorize and empower the officers of the Company to take such further action as they deem necessary or advisable in order to effectuate the foregoing resolutions and to carry out the purposes and intent thereof;

NOW THEREFORE, BE IT RESOLVED, that the officers of the Company are hereby authorized to execute and deliver such additional documents, instruments and agreements and to take such further action as they may deem necessary or advisable to effectuate the provisions of the foregoing resolutions and to carry out the intent and purposes of this Written Consent.

---

## EFFECTIVENESS

These resolutions shall be effective only to the extent they are approved by the Board of Directors of the Company as required by law or the Certificate of Incorporation of the Company.
