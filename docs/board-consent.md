ACTION BY UNANIMOUS WRITTEN CONSENT OF THE BOARD OF DIRECTORS OF [COMPANY NAME]

---

The undersigned, constituting all of the members of the Board of Directors (the "Board") of [Company Name], a Delaware corporation (the "Company"), pursuant to Sections 141(f), 242 and 245 of the Delaware General Corporation Law, hereby adopt the following resolutions by written consent:

---

RESOLVED, WHEREAS the Board of Directors deems it advisable and in the best interests of the Company and its stockholders to amend and restate the Certificate of Incorporation of the Company, and

RESOLVED, that the Certificate of Incorporation of the Company be amended and restated in substantially the form attached as Exhibit C to that certain Series Seed Preferred Stock Investment Agreement dated as of [Date] (the "Investment Agreement"), and that such amendment and restatement be, and hereby is, adopted, approved, and authorized.

---

RESOLVED, WHEREAS the Board has reviewed and considered the Investment Agreement and the proposed Series Seed Preferred Stock financing transaction contemplated thereby, and

RESOLVED, that the Investment Agreement, as presented to the Board, be, and hereby is, approved, ratified, and confirmed, and that the officers of the Company be, and hereby are, authorized to execute and deliver the Investment Agreement and all documents ancillary thereto.

---

RESOLVED, that the Company is authorized to sell and issue up to [Number] shares of Series Seed Preferred Stock at a purchase price of $[Price] per share, for an aggregate purchase price of $[Total Amount], pursuant to the Investment Agreement; and that such issuance be, and hereby is, approved, authorized, and confirmed.

---

RESOLVED, that the Company shall reserve out of its authorized but unissued shares of Common Stock [Number] shares for issuance upon the conversion of the Series Seed Preferred Stock in accordance with the terms of the amended and restated Certificate of Incorporation.

---

RESOLVED, that the Company shall use reasonable efforts to cause the shares of Series Seed Preferred Stock to qualify as "qualified small business stock" within the meaning of Section 1202 of the Internal Revenue Code of 1986, as amended, including by providing to investors such information as may be necessary for them to determine their tax treatment under Section 1202.

---

RESOLVED, that the officers of the Company are authorized to execute and deliver such other agreements and instruments as may be necessary or desirable to effectuate the Series Seed Preferred Stock financing, including without limitation the Investment Agreement and any documents ancillary thereto.

---

RESOLVED, that the Chief Executive Officer, Chief Financial Officer, and Secretary of the Company (or any other officer designated by the Board) are authorized to execute and deliver all documents and agreements contemplated by these resolutions, and that such officers be, and hereby are, authorized to take all such action as they shall deem necessary or advisable to execute the foregoing resolutions and to carry out the transactions contemplated thereby.

---

RESOLVED, that the officers of the Company are authorized to take all actions they deem necessary or advisable to carry out, give effect to, and perform all of the obligations of the Company under these resolutions and the agreements executed in connection therewith, including but not limited to the filing of the amended and restated Certificate of Incorporation with the Delaware Secretary of State, the issuance of the Series Seed Preferred Stock, and the taking of all such other steps as may be required in connection with the Series Seed Preferred Stock financing.

---

IN WITNESS WHEREOF, the undersigned have executed this Consent as of [Date].
